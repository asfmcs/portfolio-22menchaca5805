Scrumptious Coffe Cake!

Ingridients Quantity
Oil 1 Cup
Beaten eggs 2
Vanilla extract 1 tsp
Milk 1 Cup
Sugar 1 Cup
Flour 3 Cups
Baking powder 3 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Brown Sugar 1/2 Cup
Cinnamon 2 tsp
Melted Butter 1/2 Cup

Instructions

  1. In a large mixing bowl. combine oil, eggs, vanilla and milk together.
  2. In a medium bowl, blend together sugar, flour, baking powder and salt.
  3. Combine egg mixture with flour mixture. Pour half the batter into a lightly greased 9x13 pan.
  4. In a medium bowl, prepare streusel by combining brown sugar and cinnamon.
  5. Sprinkle half of streusel on top of the batter. Top with remaining batter and then sprinkle the remaining streusel on top.
  6. Drizzle with melted margarine.
  7. Bake, uncovered at 350 for 25-30 minutes.

Mouth watering Pumpkin Pie

Ingridients Quantity
Granulated Sugar 3/4 of a Cup
Ground Cinnamon 1 teaspoon
Salt 1/2 teaspoon
Ground ginger 1/2 teaspoon
Ground Cloves 1/4 teaspoon
Large Eggs 2
Pumpkin Purée 1 15 oz. Can
Evaporated milk 1 12 fl oz. can
Unbaked 9-inch deep-dish pie shell 1
Whiped Cream As Much as you Want!

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar, cinnamon, salt, ginger and cloves in small bowl.
  2. Beat eggs in large bowl.
  3. Stir in pumpkin and sugar-spice mixture.
  4. Gradually stir in evaporated milk.
  5. Pour into pie shell
  6. Bake in preheated 425° F oven for 15 minutes. Reduce temperature to 350° F; bake for 40 to 50 minutes or until knife inserted near center comes out clean.
  7. Cool on wire rack for 2 hours. Serve immediately or refrigerate. Top with whipped cream before serving.

Delicious Cooling Ice Cream!

Ingridients Quantity
Cold Heavy Whipping Cream 2 Cups
Sweetend Condensed Milk 14 ounces
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. Place sweetened condensed milk in the fridge to keep cold.
  2. Using a hand or stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, whip the cold cream on medium/high speed until soft peaks form.
  3. Turn off the machine and pour the cold condensed milk into the whipped cream.
  4. Turn the speed up to high and whisk until the mixture is thick and stiff peaks form. Turn off the machine and stir in vanilla extract (optional).
